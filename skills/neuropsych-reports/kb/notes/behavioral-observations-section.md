# Behavioral Observations Section

> Documenting patient presentation during testing to provide validity context and support diagnostic formulation.

## Related Topics
- [[validity-testing-pvt-svt]]
- [[test-results-interpretation]]

---

## Purpose

- Document how the patient presented during testing
- Provide context for test score interpretation
- Establish validity of the evaluation

---

## Elements to Include

| Category | Observations |
|----------|--------------|
| Appearance | Grooming, dress, apparent vs. stated age |
| Orientation | Person, place, time, situation |
| Motor | Gait, tremor, lateralized findings, fine motor |
| Speech/Language | Rate, rhythm, fluency, articulation, word-finding |
| Mood/Affect | Stated mood, observed affect, congruence, range |
| Effort | Motivation, cooperation, frustration tolerance |
| Attention | Sustained attention, distractibility, fatigue |
| Test behavior | Approach to tasks, response to difficulty |
| Rapport | Interpersonal style |
| Accommodations | Glasses, hearing aids |

---

## Validity Statement (Required)

```
Based on behavioral observations and formal performance validity testing,
the current results are considered a [valid / potentially limited / invalid]
estimate of [his/her/their] current neuropsychological functioning.
```

---

## Example

```
[Patient] presented as a well-groomed [sex] who appeared [his/her/their]
stated age. [He/She/They] was alert, fully oriented, and cooperative
throughout the evaluation. Speech was fluent with normal rate and prosody.
Mood was described as "[patient's words]" and affect was congruent with
full range. [He/She/They] demonstrated adequate effort and engagement.
Performance validity measures fell within acceptable limits. The current
results are considered a valid and reliable estimate of neurocognitive
functioning.
```

---

## Key Points

- Objective, behavioral descriptions
- Support validity conclusion with specific observations
- Note any factors that may affect interpretation
- Include sensory accommodations provided
