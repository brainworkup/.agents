## Neurocognitive Disorders

| Diagnosis | DSM-5-TR | ICD-10 |
|-----------|----------|--------|
| Major NCD due to Alzheimer's | — | G30.9 + F02.80/81 |
| Major NCD due to vascular | — | F01.50/51 |
| Major NCD with Lewy bodies | — | G31.83 + F02.80/81 |
| Major NCD due to TBI | — | S06.- + F02.80/81 |
| Major NCD due to frontotemporal | — | G31.09 + F02.80/81 |
| Mild NCD due to Alzheimer's | — | G30.9 + F06.70/71 |
| Mild NCD due to vascular | — | G46.8 + F06.70/71 |
| Mild NCD due to TBI | — | S06.- + F06.70/71 |

**F06.70** = without behavioral disturbance; **F06.71** = with behavioral disturbance
**F02.80** = without behavioral disturbance; **F02.81** = with behavioral disturbance
