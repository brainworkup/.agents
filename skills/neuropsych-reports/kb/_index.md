## Core Principles

1. **Evidence-Based Practice** — All recommendations and interpretations grounded in established neuropsychological research and diagnostic criteria
2. **Cultural Sensitivity** — Recognition that all tests are culturally loaded; interpretation must account for linguistic and cultural factors
3. **Validity First** — No interpretation without established validity of test performance
4. **Functional Focus** — Connect test scores to real-world functioning and practical recommendations
5. **Ethical Compliance** — Adherence to APA standards, HIPAA requirements, and test security protocols
