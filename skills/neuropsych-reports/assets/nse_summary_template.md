# NSE Transcript Summary Template

Use this template when summarizing a Neurobehavioral Status Exam (NSE) transcript into the Background/History sections of a neuropsychological report.

---

## Instructions

1. Read the entire transcript before beginning
2. Use Chain of Density (CoD) approach for each section
3. Integrate information regardless of where it appeared in the transcript
4. Write 1–3 pages total
5. Third person, past tense throughout
6. Professional, neutral clinical tone
7. Focus on information relevant to the chief complaint and referral question

## Template Notes

**Source Attribution:** When information comes from multiple sources, note the source:
- "Per patient report..."
- "According to [collateral informant]..."
- "Medical records indicate..."
- "Review of prior evaluation from [date] revealed..."

**Discrepancies:** When sources disagree, document both perspectives:
- "[Patient] reported X; however, [collateral] stated Y."

**Gaps:** Note when important information was not available:
- "Birth and early developmental history was not available for review."

**Quotation:** Include patient quotes when clinically illustrative:
- "[Patient] described [his/her/their] memory as '[direct quote].'"
