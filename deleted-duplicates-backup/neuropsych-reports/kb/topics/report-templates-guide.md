## Full Evaluation Template

**Best for:** Differential diagnosis, treatment planning, academic/vocational accommodations, disability determination

**Structure:**
- Complete 8-section report
- Comprehensive background
- Full cognitive battery
- Detailed recommendations

**Length:** 8–20 pages

**See:** [[full-evaluation-template-usage]]

---

## Brief Assessment Template

**Best for:** Screening evaluations, competency screenings, bedside cognitive evaluations

**Structure:**
- Abbreviated background
- Screening measures only
- Focused recommendations

**Length:** 3–6 pages

**See:** [[brief-assessment-template-usage]]

---

## Pediatric Template

**Best for:** Children and adolescents

**Specialized Sections:**
- Developmental history (expanded)
- Teacher/school input
- Adaptive behavior
- Educational recommendations (IEP/504 planning)
- Developmental milestones

**See:** [[pediatric-evaluation-considerations]]

---

## Geriatric/Dementia Template

**Best for:** Older adults with suspected cognitive decline

**Specialized Sections:**
- Collateral interview summary
- Functional assessment (ADLs/IADLs)
- Capacity considerations
- Staging (CDR, GDS, FAST)
- Caregiver input

**See:** [[geriatric-evaluation-considerations]]

---

## Forensic Template

**Best for:** Legal proceedings, IMEs, disability evaluations

**Specialized Sections:**
- Heightened validity testing documentation
- Daubert/Frye admissibility considerations
- Functional capacity statements
- Malingering assessment
- Role clarification statement

**See:** [[forensic-evaluation-standards]]

---

## NSE Summary Template

**Best for:** Summarizing Neurobehavioral Status Exam transcripts

**Focus:**
- 1–3 pages
- Reason for Referral + Background/History
- Information relevant to chief complaint
- Note discrepancies between sources

**See:** [[nse-summary-template-usage]]
